Solar Power Solution

Solar systems can be installed on the roofs of residential, industrial and commercial complexes, housing societies, educational institutes, community centers, government organizations, private institutions etc. The solar panels can also be installed on ground, terraces and on parking sheds too. No additional FSI is utilized. Due to solar PV panels array on roof, roof remains cooler and in turn the temperature of the floor below gets reduced by a degree Celsius, thereby reducing the load on the air conditioning equipment and saving electricity. Further, if solar PV panels are installed on raised fabrication structure (super structure), a spare hall is created on the roof without consuming any FSI.In many cases the super structure of solar plant, helps resolving water proofing issues.
